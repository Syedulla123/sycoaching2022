WHO I AM

Hey, I’m Sneha! I’ve been into health and fitness for most of my life. I played a lot of sports growing up, such as soccer, track, swimming, and tennis. I have a strong passion for exercise and fitness, and I am always diving deeper into the field to increase my knowledge. I believe fitness can improve one's life in so many aspects—mentally, physically, and spiritually.

MY JOURNEY

About two years ago, I started bodybuilding. I transformed my physique from underweight to muscular. I learned all about the proper diet needed to gain weight, lose weight, or maintain weight while improving body composition. I have direct experience with bulking, cutting, and recomping. Fitness has completely changed my life from a self-respect, confidence, and health aspect, and I want to help others achieve the same.

WHAT I CAN DO FOR YOU

At SY Coaching, the support is real and it starts the moment we meet. I don’t have a one-size-fits-all approach, but rather, I develop a customized plan just for you. It is a total fitness experience designed around your abilities, your body, and your goals. It can be anything from a specific weight loss goal to a muscle-building goal. Your plan will contain a 360 approach to your health, from training to nutrition to sleep. The first step? Contact me today so we can get started!
